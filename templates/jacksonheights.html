Information about Jackson Heights

This is my neighborhood, so I thought I knew a lot. I thought the neighborhood was a strong green, but it was actually a yellow. It was a ratio of 17, higher than the city's average. I guess it is because I live next to an Asian supermarket, but then if I think about that and another supermarket right next to each other are very big stores where half the community goes. There are parts of my community that don't have supermarkets so they have to travel a good amount. However, I never noticed the number of bodegas in my community, but as I walked I realized that the data was indeed not lying.
