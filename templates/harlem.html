Information about Harlem

In most of Manhattan, the area is green. But once you start going north, near Harlem and Washington Heights, it changes from a green to an orange. It goes to show the amount of difference in every community and how even though it is surrounded by areas with good access, the communitiy does not.

Harlem has a huge history, and is a big part of the construction of culture and life in New York City and in the whole country. To not help those in areas of such great access, it is disrespectful to not only the people living in there now, but for the history and the past.
